Hackathon Day-wise Plan

  • 8th March
    Registration Window
    This is the official registration period for participants to sign up for the competition. All interested teams must complete their registration before 14th March to be eligible.
  • 14th March
    Registration Closed
    No further registrations will be accepted after this date. Teams that have successfully registered will receive further details about the competition process and guidelines.
  • 19th March
    Idea Pitch
    Teams will present their innovative and brainstorming ideas, explaining the problem statement, proposed solution, and feasibility. A PPT with at most 5 slides is to be presented in this phase.
  • 26th March
    Prototype Design & Simulation
    The teams will develop a functional prototype or simulation of their idea. This phase focuses on technical implementation, feasibility testing, and initial validation.
  • 10th April
    Final Demonstration & Feedback
    Teams will showcase the final version of their prototype, demonstrating its functionality. Judges will provide feedback, and winners will be announced based on innovation, execution, and impact.
